Melanotan II

Melanotan II

Also known as: MT-II, MT2, Barbie Drug

High Risk
Skin & HairSexual Health

A synthetic melanocyte-stimulating hormone analog. Induces tanning without UV. Viral as "Barbie drug" but carries significant safety concerns including melanoma risk. ILLEGAL in US and UK.

Benefits

Induces tanning within ~5 doses without UV
Increases sexual arousal and libido
May provide some UV protection via melanin

Side Effects & Risks

Nausea and vomiting (common)moderate
Facial flushingmild
Increased moles and frecklesmoderate
SERIOUS: Potential melanoma/skin cancer riskserious
SERIOUS: Renal infarction (potentially fatal)serious
SERIOUS: Brain swelling reportedserious

Dosing Information

Typical Dose

250-500 mcg initially, maintenance 500 mcg/week

Administration

Subcutaneous injection

Cycle Length

Loading then maintenance

Notes

NON-SELECTIVE melanocortin agonist. Primary safety concern.

Legal Status

FDA Status

NOT FDA-approved; illegal to purchase in US and UK

Availability

Sold illegally online despite bans

WADA Status

Banned by WADA (athletes beware)

Evidence

Evidence LevelModerate

Development abandoned due to safety concerns. Health agencies globally advise against use.
