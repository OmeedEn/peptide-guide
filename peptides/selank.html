Selank

Selank (TP-7)

Also known as: TP-7

Low Risk
CognitiveSleep & WellnessImmune Support

A synthetic analog of the immunomodulatory peptide tuftsin. Registered as anxiolytic in Russia. Modulates GABAergic and serotonergic pathways for calm without sedation.

Benefits

Reduces anxiety without sedation or cognitive impairment
Improves emotional stability and stress resilience
Enhances immune function
Nootropic effects (memory, learning)
Modulates GABA and serotonin pathways
Helpful for withdrawal support

Side Effects & Risks

Generally very well toleratedmild
Mild fatigue in some usersmild
Nasal irritation (intranasal route)mild

Dosing Information

Typical Dose

250-500 mcg/day

Administration

Intranasal spray or subcutaneous injection

Cycle Length

5-14 day cycles

Notes

Morning administration recommended. Effects build over 4-12 weeks.

Legal Status

FDA Status

Not FDA-approved in USA

Availability

Removed from Category 2 Sept 2024; registered in Russia

WADA Status

Not currently banned

Evidence

Evidence LevelModerate

Decades of research and clinical use in Russia. Multiple published studies. Not studied in large-scale Western trials.
