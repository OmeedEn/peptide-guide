SS-31 (Elamipretide)

Also known as: Elamipretide, Bendavia

A mitochondria-targeting tetrapeptide that binds to cardiolipin. Optimizes mitochondrial structure and function. FDA-approved for Barth syndrome (Sept 2025).

Reverses age-related mitochondrial decline
Increases muscle mass and fatigue resistance

Supports kidney and neurological function
